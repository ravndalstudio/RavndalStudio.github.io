About Checklists Pro+

Tired of simple to-do lists? Checklists Pro+ is for those who need to remember how a task is done, not just that it exists.

It lets you create **reusable checklists** for places, routines, and situations you return to frequently:

  • Cabin opening or closing, Boat launch or winter prep.
  • Travel departure or return, Vehicle maintenance.
  • Worksite tasks, Weekly or monthly routines.

Intelligent Workflow

Link a checklist to a GPS location or address. **When you arrive, it is ready.** No searching. No rebuilding old lists.

Every list is reusable. You do not lose it when you complete it. Just reset and use again.

Why Checklists Pro+ Helps

  • Prevent mistakes in tasks you do infrequently.
  • Reduce mental load and build consistency across people.
  • Make handoffs easy and stop rebuilding the same list from memory.

Key Features

  • Location triggered visibility (optional) with radius control for nearby detection.
  • Manual or automatic reset.
  • Clear step-by-step layout and option to share lists with others.
  • Works offline after setup.
  • Privacy friendly (only what you enter) and designed for calm reliability.

**WHEN IT APPEARS:** Arrive at the cabin. The cabin checklist is there. Hook up the trailer. The towing checklist is there. Park at the marina. The launch checklist is there.
